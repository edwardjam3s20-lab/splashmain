This Privacy Policy explains how KINGJAMES TECHNOLOGIES collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use SplashPass. It complies with the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019 and the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Introduction

Welcome to SplashPass — a car wash booking and payments platform operated by KINGJAMES TECHNOLOGIES, a business entity registered in Kenya ("we", "us", "our", or "SplashPass").

This Privacy Policy applies to your use of the SplashPass mobile application ("the App"), our website at splashpass.site, and any related services (collectively, "the Services").

By using the Services, you confirm that you have read, understood, and agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use immediately.

This Policy is designed to comply with:

  • The Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019 (DPA 2019) and the Data Protection (General) Regulations, 2021
  • The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) — EU Regulation 2016/679 — as your data is stored on servers in Paris, France
  • Applicable consumer protection and electronic communications laws in Kenya

3. Personal Data We Collect

3.1 Account & Identity Data

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (used for M-Pesa payments and SMS confirmations)
  • Password (stored in hashed form — we never store plain-text passwords)

3.2 Vehicle Data

  • Car make, model, and year
  • Vehicle colour
  • Number plate / registration number
  • Vehicle type (Saloon, SUV, Pickup, etc.)

3.3 Location Data

With your permission, we access your device's GPS coordinates to show nearby wash points on the map. We do not store your location history — location data is processed in real time and is not retained on our servers.

3.4 Booking & Transaction Data

  • Selected wash point, service type, date and time slot
  • Booking status and booking reference codes
  • Payment amounts and M-Pesa transaction references
  • Operator payout amounts and platform commission splits

3.5 Subscription & Loyalty Data

  • Subscription plan and status (Trial, Active, Expired)
  • Subscription start date and renewal date
  • Loyalty points balance and tier (Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum)

3.6 Usage & Technical Data

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • Feature interactions (e.g. screens visited, buttons tapped) for debugging purposes only
  • Error logs

4. How We Collect Your Data

  • Directly from you when you register, complete onboarding, add a car, make a booking, or contact us
  • Automatically when you use the App (location data, device data)
  • From Safaricom M-Pesa in the form of payment confirmation callbacks
  • From Africa's Talking in the form of SMS message delivery reports

5. How We Use Your Data

  • Account Management — To create and manage your account, verify your identity, and provide customer support. Contract performance
  • Booking Services — To process bookings, generate QR wash passes, allocate time slots, and send confirmations. Contract performance
  • Payments — To initiate M-Pesa STK Push prompts, receive payment confirmations, and calculate payouts. Contract performance
  • SMS Notifications — To send booking confirmation messages to your phone. Legitimate interest
  • Location Services — To display wash points near you on the map. Consent
  • Loyalty Programme — To track and award loyalty points and manage rewards. Contract performance
  • Service Improvement — To analyse usage patterns and diagnose technical issues. Legitimate interest
  • Legal Compliance — To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and court orders. Legal obligation

6. How We Share Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third parties for marketing purposes. We share data only as follows:

6.1 Service Providers (Data Processors)

  • Supabase Inc. — Our primary database and backend. Your data is stored in Paris, France (West EU). Data processed under a data processing agreement.
  • Vercel Inc. — Our application hosting provider.
  • Safaricom PLC (M-Pesa) — Your phone number and payment amount are shared to initiate and confirm M-Pesa payments. Safaricom's own privacy policy applies.
  • Africa's Talking Ltd — Your phone number and booking details are shared to deliver SMS confirmations. Their privacy policy applies.
  • CARTO / Leaflet.js — Map tiles are loaded from CartoDB's servers. Your approximate location may be included in tile requests but is not stored by us.

6.2 Wash Point Operators

When you make a booking, your name, vehicle details (make, model, plate, type), selected service, date, time, and booking reference are shared with the relevant wash point operator to facilitate your wash. Operators are bound by our Operator Terms of Service.

6.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your data if required by law, court order, or government authority, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of SplashPass, our users, or the public.

7. Data Storage & International Transfers

Your personal data is stored in Supabase's West EU (Paris, France) region — within the European Union and subject to GDPR standards.

As a Kenyan business storing data in the EU, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place consistent with Kenya's DPA 2019 requirements for cross-border data transfers and GDPR Article 46 standard contractual clauses.

Vercel may process request metadata on servers in various regions globally under Vercel's standard data processing terms.

9. Your Rights

Under Kenya's Data Protection Act 2019 and the GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification — Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure — Request deletion of your account and personal data, subject to legal retention requirements
  • Right to Restrict Processing — Request that we limit how we use your data in certain circumstances
  • Right to Data Portability — Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to Object — Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to Withdraw Consent — Where processing is based on consent (e.g. location), withdraw it at any time via your device settings
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint — File a complaint with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC) in Kenya or the relevant EU supervisory authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at splashpass.site@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

10. Account Deletion

You may delete your SplashPass account at any time directly within the App:

  • Navigate to Account → Settings
  • Tap "Delete My Account"
  • Confirm your identity and the deletion request
  • Your account and personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days

Booking records and financial transaction data will be retained for up to 7 years as required by Kenyan financial regulations, but will be anonymised so they cannot be linked back to you personally.

You may also request deletion by emailing splashpass.site@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" from your registered email address.

11. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data, including:

  • All data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Supabase Row-Level Security (RLS) policies ensure users can only access their own data
  • Passwords are never stored in plain text
  • API keys and credentials are stored as environment variables and never exposed in client-side code
  • Two-factor authentication is enforced for administrative access
  • M-Pesa API credentials are stored securely server-side and never transmitted to the client

While we take reasonable precautions, no system is completely secure. In the event of a data breach posing a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify you and the relevant authorities as required by law.

12. Minimum Age Requirement

SplashPass is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under 18. If you are under 18, please do not register or use the Services.

If we become aware that we have collected data from a person under 18, we will immediately delete that information. If you believe we may have collected data from a minor, please contact us at splashpass.site@gmail.com.

13. Cookies & Local Storage

The SplashPass App is a Progressive Web App (PWA) and does not use traditional tracking cookies. We use the browser's localStorage mechanism solely to:

  • Store your session information after login (to keep you signed in)
  • Cache app preferences and settings locally on your device

This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to third parties. You can clear it at any time by logging out or clearing your browser's storage.
