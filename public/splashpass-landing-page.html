Why SplashPass

A better way to get your car washed.

No more queuing, no more guessing prices, no more cash fumbling. SplashPass brings the entire car wash experience into your phone.

01

Book ahead of time

Choose your preferred car wash, pick a time that works for you, and arrive to a ready bay. Your car doesn't wait. You don't either.

Available at all partner washes
02

Pay securely with M-Pesa

Pay via Lipa na M-Pesa before you arrive. No handling cash at the wash bay. A confirmed receipt lands in your messages immediately.

Instant M-Pesa confirmation
03

Scan and go

Show your SplashPass QR code at arrival. The attendant scans it, your booking is verified, and your wash begins. No paper, no receipts, no friction.

Zero paperwork
04

Earn with every wash

How it works

From app to bay in three steps.

The whole process takes under two minutes. The wash takes care of itself.

1

Choose your wash

Open SplashPass, see every car wash near you with live pricing, ratings, and available time slots. Pick the one that fits.

2

Pay and confirm

Tap to pay via M-Pesa. You'll get an STK push, enter your PIN, and receive your booking confirmation instantly. Done before you start the engine.

3

Scan and enjoy

Arrive, show your SplashPass QR. The attendant scans it in seconds. Your booking is verified automatically — no receipts, no disputes.
